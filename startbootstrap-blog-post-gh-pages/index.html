About Me

by Paul Kim

Posted on May 16, 2019

Taking this course has opened my eyes and brain to create as an artist behind a computer. With so many tools available to use not only the ones taught in this course makes being a computer artist that much more intriguing and exciting.

If it were not for professor Williams to teach the course in such a way where it captivated my attention, I do not think I would have had this same feeling for computer science/programming.

There was never a dull moment since the start of the course, and after every class, it left me wanting to know more.

I plan to continue my degree for Business and Information Systems
